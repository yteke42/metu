Frequently Asked Questions

How does "Your Digital Agenda" works?

You need to register and login first. Then you can fill your agenda as you wish. Choose the date and start writing!


What should I do if I forget my password?

You can reset your password using the "password reset number" provided during registration.


Can others see what I have saved in my agenda?

No. The content of each account is specific to that account only. No one else can access your data.


What is your mission?

Our mission is providing people a useful website that they can keep their information inside. "Your personal agenda" has many uses like agenda, diary, or even reminder of events. We will be keep upgrading the website and adding new features in the future.
