The Matrix (1999) is a sci-fi action film directed by the Wachowskis. It follows Neo, a hacker who discovers that reality is a simulated world created by intelligent machines to subdue humanity. With the help of Morpheus and Trinity, Neo joins the fight to free mankind, confronting powerful agents and uncovering his own destiny as "The One." The film explores themes of reality, control, and freedom, and is known for its groundbreaking visual effects, including "bullet time" action sequences.
