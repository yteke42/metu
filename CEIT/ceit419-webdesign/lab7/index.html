Classic Margherita Pizza

About the Dish

The Margherita pizza is a classic Italian dish known for its simplicity and fresh ingredients: tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 220Â°C.
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella slices and drizzle olive oil on top.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.
